隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026年6月24日

GWPlayer（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护用户的隐私。本隐私政策说明了本应用如何收集、使用和披露您的信息。

信息收集

本应用不收集任何个人身份信息。具体说明如下：

  • 本地文件访问：本应用仅在您主动导入音频文件时，将文件复制到设备的本地沙盒存储中。这些文件始终保留在您的设备上，不会上传至任何服务器。
  • 用户偏好设置：本应用使用 iOS 系统提供的 UserDefaults 在本地存储您的主题偏好（浅色/深色/跟随系统），该数据不会离开您的设备。
  • 数据库：本应用使用本地 SQLite 数据库管理播放列表和曲目元数据，所有数据均存储在设备本地。

数据使用

  • 本应用不包含任何第三方分析、广告或追踪SDK。
  • 本应用不会将您的任何数据上传至远程服务器。
  • 本应用不要求创建账户或登录。

数据共享

本应用不共享任何用户数据。由于所有数据均存储在本地设备上，本应用没有能力将您的数据分享给任何第三方。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。本应用不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有重大变更，我们会在应用更新时通知您。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

GWPlayer ("the App") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information Collection

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information:

  • Local File Access: The App copies audio files to the device's local sandbox storage only when you actively import them. These files remain on your device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • User Preferences: The App uses iOS UserDefaults to store your theme preference (light/dark/system) locally on your device.
  • Database: The App uses a local SQLite database to manage playlists and track metadata. All data is stored locally.

Data Usage

  • The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • The App does not upload any of your data to remote servers.
  • The App does not require account creation or sign-in.

Data Sharing

The App does not share any user data. Since all data is stored locally, the App has no ability to share your data with any third party.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through app updates.
